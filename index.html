MC Durians is located at Sungai Klau, Raub Pahang, Malaysia. We have 30 years of experience in the art of durian cultivation dating back to the 1990s.

  • Can provide up to five hundred tons of durian per month.
  • Have a dozen units of the most advanced liquid nitrogen freezers (-100C).
  • Possess government hygienic specification certificates and international authoritative certificates, such as: Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP), HALAL certification, etc

Our company had achieved leapfrog development and is leading the export of durian to China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Indonesia, Japan, Singapore, Britain, Australia, the United States, Canada, New Zealand and other countries.
